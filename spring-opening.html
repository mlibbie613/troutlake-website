Spring Opening

Everything we do to get your property ready before your first visit of the season.

Every spring, before you arrive for your first visit of the season, our team performs a thorough opening of your property. We check everything from plumbing to appliances to the yard so you can show up and enjoy your time — no surprises, no worries.

🔧

Plumbing Check

Inspect all plumbing for leaks, damage, or issues that may have occurred over the winter months. We check under sinks, around toilets, and at all water connections.

🌡️

HVAC System Check

Test heating and cooling systems, replace filters, and ensure everything is running efficiently before the season begins.

🍽️

Appliance Check

Test all major appliances — refrigerator, stove, dishwasher, washer/dryer — to confirm they are in working order before your arrival.

⚡

Minor Electrical Check

Check outlets, switches, interior and exterior lighting, and identify any electrical concerns that may need attention.

🏠

Exterior Inspection

Walk the full exterior for any winter damage, including roof, siding, foundation, windows, and doors. We document and report anything that needs attention.

🍃

Gutter Check

Inspect and clear gutters of leaves and debris from the fall and winter season to ensure proper drainage.

🧹

Cleaning

Interior and exterior cleaning so your property is fresh and ready for your first visit of the season.

🌿

Yard Cleanup

Clear winter debris, rake leaves, and tidy up the grounds so your yard is looking its best when you arrive.

📋

Detailed Report

A full written report with photos documenting the condition of your property, delivered to you before your first visit so you know exactly what to expect.

Ready to get started?
