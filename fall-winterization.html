Fall Winterization

Everything we do to protect your property before the cold Wisconsin winter sets in.

At the end of every season, our team performs a thorough winterization of your property. We make sure everything is properly shut down, protected, and secured so your home makes it through the winter in great shape — and is ready for you again in the spring.

🔧

Winterize Plumbing & Appliances

Properly winterize all plumbing and appliances to prevent freezing and damage during the cold Wisconsin winter months.

🌡️

HVAC Temperature Set

Set your heating system to the appropriate temperature to protect your property through the winter without wasting energy.

🏠

Exterior Inspection

Walk the full exterior to identify and address any vulnerabilities before winter weather arrives, including windows, doors, and the roof.

🧹

Cleaning

Interior and exterior cleaning to leave your property in great condition and ready for when you return in the spring.

🗑️

Trash Removal

Remove all trash and debris from the property so nothing is left behind over the winter months.

🍂

Yard Pickup

Clear the yard of leaves, debris, and any items that need to be stored or removed before the snow arrives.

🔒

Secure the Property

Make sure all doors, windows, and access points are properly secured before we leave for the season.

📋

Detailed Report

A full written report with photos documenting the condition of your property at close, delivered to you so you have peace of mind all winter long.

Ready to get started?

Fall winterization is included in all Trout Lake Home Services plans.
